# Public-beta legal, privacy, and safety surfaces

**Professional review required:** this product surface has not been reviewed or approved by legal counsel, privacy counsel, compliance specialists, or licensed clinicians.

## User-facing disclosures to publish before launch
- US, FOR REAL is relationship-wellness software, not emergency services, not medical care, not legal advice, and not a substitute for licensed therapy.
- The Guide is AI-assisted and can be wrong. Members should correct, reject, or remove AI-derived themes.
- Crisis/emergency limitation: if someone may be in immediate danger, call local emergency services or a crisis hotline.
- Therapy-cost comparisons are educational estimates only, configurable by `COUPLES_THERAPY_RATE_MIN_USD` and `COUPLES_THERAPY_RATE_MAX_USD`; they are not bills, guarantees, or claims of equivalent care.
- Private answers remain private. With consent, sanitized unattributed themes may influence prompts shown to the answer owner or partner, but raw wording must never be shown to the partner.

## Data retention and deletion policy
- Private records: owner exportable/deletable; hidden from partner and shared dossier.
- Shared couple records: visible to both active members; deletion of couple history requires two-sided confirmation unless a legal/safety exception is documented.
- Operational logs: metadata only, no relationship text, default retention target 30 days unless incident hold is required.
- Legal requests/subpoenas: preserve only after validated legal review; disclose minimum required metadata/content; notify users when legally permitted.

## Clinical-review checklist
- Crisis language and escalation paths.
- Abuse/coercive-control handling.
- Unsafe joint-session conversion to separate guidance.
- Accountability language without diagnosis or blame balancing.
- Homework safety and consent.
